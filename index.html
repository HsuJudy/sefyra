Questions

Good to know.

What is Sefyra?

A calm, private task manager for iPhone built on a capture → clarify → do rhythm. Get every thought out of your head, then trust the system to surface only what matters now.

Is Sefyra free?

Yes — Sefyra is free to download on the App Store and works fully offline.

Is my data private?

Completely. Your notes and tasks live on your device. There are no ads, no trackers, and no account required to start.

What makes it different?

Instead of a guilt-pile of everything, Sefyra filters by your context, time, and energy so you see only what you can actually do this moment.
