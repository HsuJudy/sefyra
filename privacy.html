Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Sefyra does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account. The app does not require sign-in.
  • No tracking or analytics. We do not use any third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.
  • On-device only. Everything you capture — inbox items, next actions, projects, waiting-for, someday, reference, and your completed history — is stored locally on your device. It is never sent to us or anyone else.
  • Calendar. If you choose “Add to Calendar,” the app creates an event in your own Apple Calendar using write-only access. We never read your calendar.

Because we collect no data, there is nothing for us to sell, lose, or disclose.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the “Last updated” date above will change with it.
