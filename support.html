Common questions

Where is my data?

On your device. Sefyra has no account and sends nothing to a server.

How do I sort something in my inbox?

On the Clarify tab, drag a card toward a label — ← Trash, → Actionable, ↑ Someday, ↓ Reference.

How do I check off a task?

Tap the circle next to it on the Next or Now list. For a project, open it and check the highlighted next step.

How do I edit or reorder a list item?

Tap it to edit. Swipe left to delete. Tap Edit to drag-reorder.

Where do completed items go?

The Done list (last chip in Lists) keeps a running history of everything you’ve finished, grouped by day.

Want to just try it?

In the Review tab, tap “Load sample data” to fill the app with an example setup you can explore.
